Estate Management

Easy, Quick, Handy

Purposes Are Clear and Simple

One Stop Solution for Estate Management

Include two software:

  1. Web platform for estate manager
  2. Mobile app for tenant

Estate Managers Happy

Send notices to tenants. Auto notify to the Tenant App (Android and iOS)

Estate managers very easily send PDF notices to different audiences (owners, tenants, occupants, or agents) who own residences, carparks, or shops

Your Tenants Happy

Amenities Booking on the Tenant App

Estate Managers can customize the amenities, photos, opening & closing hours, booking sections, booking fees and more...

Tenant Marketplace

Estate Managers can publish the marketing Ads

For example, recommend the residence to the home insurance company. Or which grocery store is on sale this month.

Serverless Edge

Application runs on serverless global network

The servers are no single point of failure. Which include application and database servers. Serves in the fastest performance no matter whether your estate is located anywhere in the globe.

Data Protection and Privacy

Security in mind for system development

All data transmission, Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) and sensitive data are all protected by encryption.

Free to use and Fully Open Sources

Both the admin webapp and the tenant mobile app are open sources

The two codesets publish on GitHub. 100% transparent. For developers, you can fork the repo and deploy it to your Cloudflare Workers account. For non-technical users, you can free to use my Cloudflare Workers Platform immediately
